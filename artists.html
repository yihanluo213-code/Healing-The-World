Professional Artists

Experienced artists guiding and elevating the dialogue through mentorship, craft, and artistic leadership.

Profile Format

Each artist profile is intended to include the artist's work, name, category, school or affiliation when relevant, and a short 30-50 word introduction.

  • Artist name and category
  • Artwork image or representative work
  • Grade, school, affiliation, or short biography
  • Optional contact or social media when approved
